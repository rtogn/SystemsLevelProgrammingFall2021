getMostFrequentChar.c will count the most frequent character occuring in a text file.

compile: gcc getMostFrequentChar.c 
run: 
	**Argument 1 must be the name of a txt file.**
	./a.out belogus.txt

output:
	The most frequent letter is 'a'. It appeard 24 times.

The other two files (addressOfArray.c and addressOfScalars.c) have no input requirements and are
assignments for learning about pointers. Just compile and run as normal to view output.
